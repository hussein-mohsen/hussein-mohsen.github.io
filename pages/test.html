Bio


Hussein Mohsen is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the Carrot-Zhang and Morris Labs at MSKCC. He is interested in computational method development, trans-population cancer genomics, and investigating the embedded legacies of racism in genomic practice. Hussein was a Scholar of the CCNY-MSKCC Partnership Program and a recipient of an AACR Scholar-in-Training Award and a Fulbright Graduate Scholarship. He received his PhD in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics and MA in History of Science and Medicine at Yale University and BS in Computer Science at the Lebanese American University.
