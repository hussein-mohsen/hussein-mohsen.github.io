Hussein Mohsen

Postdoctoral Research Fellow · MSKCC · He/him

Bio


Hussein Mohsen is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Morris and Carrot-Zhang Labs at the Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. His research focuses on computational method development, trans-population genomics, and investigating the embedded legacies of racism in genomic practice. He is a recipient of a Fulbright Graduate Scholarship, AACR Scholar-in-Training Award, and Gruber Science Fellowship. Dr. Mohsen received his PhD in Computational Biology and Bioinformatics and MA in History of Science and Medicine at Yale University and BS in Computer Science at the Lebanese American University.
